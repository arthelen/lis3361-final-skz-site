Group

Here are a few fun facts about the whole group of Stray Kids!

- They became million sellers after their "NOEASY" comeback in August 2021 and were the first in their company (JYP Entertainment) to do so (sold over 1 million copies - had 930,000 pre-orders and sold 641,589 in its first week)

- After performing a Deadpool inspired stage on MNET's "Kingdom: Legendary War" TV series, Ryan Reynolds (who plays Deadpool) personally reached out to them and even sent Bang Chan his autograph along with a few items

- Fans of the group are called "STAY" which was derived from the word "Stray" in their name - the "r" stands for "reason" and represents the fans being their reason and where they stay

- They are a self-producing group (this is usually unheard of in K-pop as many groups usually have most of their songs made for them, but this is starting to change and become more popular)

- Their name is often abbreviated to SKZ by their fans

- 3RACHA (mentioned on the home page - their producing team made of 3 of the members), originally started on the music platform SoundCloud before Stray Kids were formed

- Most (if not all) of the members can speak and understand English quite well, with 2 of them (Bang Chan and Felix) having it as their native language

- They were both the 3rd most streamed Korean Male Group and 4th most streamed Korean artist on the music application Spotify in 2021
